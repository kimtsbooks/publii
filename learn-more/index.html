Feeling All My Feelings Collection

What These Books Are

Books that help little kids understand and manage big emotions

Feeling All My Feelings collection is a set of gentle, rhyming books designed to help young children recognize, name, and talk about big emotions like anger, fear, worry, shyness, and grief — with the support of a caring adult.

These books are not traditional storybooks. They’re meant to be read together, slowly and repeatedly, as tools for emotional understanding and connection.

Who These Books Are For

Designed for young children- and the adults who support them.

  • Toddlers and preschoolers learning to name feelings
  • Parents and caregivers navigating big emotions at home
  • Teachers, therapists, and counselors supporting emotional development
  • Any adult who wants a simple, age-appropriate way to talk about feelings

What Makes This Collection Different

What Makes This Collection Different

Each book uses simple rhymes and reassuring language to validate children’s feelings without minimizing them or rushing them away. The goal is not to “fix” emotions, but to help children feel seen, understood, and safe while learning how emotions work.
